Aras Community "Type" Definition
Open:The project software is available to anyone.   It may be downloaded and used for no charge.   Each project has a license agreement controlling the usage. 
Subscriber Only:This solution may only be used by companies with an active Aras Subscription.  
Commercial:The solution is available only with a license or usage fee.  Note that some of the Commercial projects may also include Open solutions (versions that may have feature limations, but are available for no charge) or  trial versions (versions with user limits or time-outs that may be downloaded and used wihout charge).   The commercial, production version of the solutions must be purchased from the Author.
