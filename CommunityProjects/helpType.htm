Aras Community "Type" Definition
Open:The project software is available "as-is".   It may be downloaded and used by anyone for no charge. 
Exclusive:Aras Unlimited Support subscribers may download the full production version of the solution and deploy it for no cost.
Commercial:The solution is available only with a license or usage fee.  Note that many of the Commercial projects also include Open solutions (versions that may have feature limations, but are available for no charge) or  trial versions (versions with user limits or time-outs that may be downloaded and used wihout charge).   The commercial, production version of the solutions must be purchased from the Author.
