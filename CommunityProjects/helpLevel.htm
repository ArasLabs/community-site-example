Aras Community "Level" Definition
As-Is:The solution is available 'as-is' and has not reviewed by Aras in any way
Registered:The solution is registered with Aras to ensure that no namespace conflicts occur with other Registered, Certified, or Managed solutions.
Certified:The solution is qualified by Aras as compliant to the certification criteria and support is provided by the contributing organization.
Managed:The solution is managed directly by the Aras test release process and hotline support is available for customers on an active Aras Unlimited subscription package.
