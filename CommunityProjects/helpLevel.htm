Aras Community "Level" Definition
Unknown:The solution is distributed "as-is". It has not been tested by Aras
Verified:The Solution has been confirmed by Aras Corp as a running in production at a company
Registered:The Solution is being used in production by at least one company, and the package has been tested to not conflict with any other Registered or Certified solutions.
Certified:The Solution is tested by Aras QA and customers on Unlimited Subscriptions can call the hot-line for support.
